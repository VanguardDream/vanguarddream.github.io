# Development of Control Algorithms for Performance Evaluation and Functional Testing of Sampling Manipulators in Unmanned Ground Vehicles

**Funded by:** KAERI (Korea Atomic Energy Research Institute)  
**Period:** 2022 – 2023  
**Keywords:** Manipulator control, UGV, Functional testing, Ground sampling

---

## Overview

This project developed **control algorithms for a sampling manipulator** mounted on an Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV), targeting performance evaluation and functional testing in field-deployable conditions. The system is intended for ground sample collection in environments inaccessible or hazardous to humans.

## Key Contributions

- Defined motion sequences and performance evaluation metrics for functional testing
- Designed control algorithms and established the testing environment and procedures
- Analyzed test data and addressed issues related to vibration, trajectory error, and repeatability
