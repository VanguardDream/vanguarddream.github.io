# Freedom of Movement: A Drivetrain Study for Future Autonomous Mobility

**Funded by:** Hyundai Motor Group  
**Period:** 2022 – 2023  
**Keywords:** Mobility, Drivetrain, Vehicle dynamics, Autonomous mobility

---

## Overview

This project investigated **novel drivetrain concepts and performance trade-offs** for future autonomous mobility platforms — exploring how alternative drivetrain configurations can expand the operational envelope of self-driving vehicles beyond conventional constraints.

## Key Contributions

- Defined driving and actuation scenarios and derived corresponding performance requirements
- Constructed a dynamics-based performance comparison framework across drivetrain configurations
- Summarized design-alternative trade-offs to guide downstream engineering decisions
